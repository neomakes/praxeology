## How to Read This System

Governance tiers cascade downward. Higher tiers take precedence over lower ones.
A Procedure cannot contradict its Doctrine. A Playbook cannot contradict its Procedure.

```
Tier 1 STRATEGY  — WHY we exist (founding-level, rarely changes)
Tier 2 DOCTRINE  — WHAT we will/will not do (board-level, changes with major pivots)
Tier 3 PROCEDURE — HOW we allocate resources (executive-level, quarterly review)
Tier 4 PLAYBOOK  — PATTERNS we repeat (team-level, monthly review)
Exec   WORK PLAN — NOW what we are doing (individual, daily/weekly)
```
